How to Enroll

Step 1: Contact Us

Visit our office, call us, or fill out the online inquiry form. Our counselors will discuss your learning goals, assess your current level, and recommend the most suitable course for you.

Step 2: Take a Placement Test (if applicable)

For General English courses, we conduct a free placement test to determine your current proficiency level. This ensures you're placed in the right class for optimal learning. IELTS courses don't require a placement test.

Step 3: Complete Registration

Fill out the registration form with your personal details and select your preferred batch timing. Provide necessary documents (photo ID, recent photograph). Our staff will guide you through the process.

Step 4: Make Payment

Pay the course fee via your preferred method (cash, bank transfer, or mobile banking). You'll receive an official receipt and payment confirmation. Installment plans available upon request.

Step 5: Receive Study Materials

On your first day of class, you'll receive all necessary study materials including textbooks, workbooks, and access to our online learning platform. Your learning journey begins!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I switch batches after enrollment?

Yes, batch transfers are possible subject to seat availability in your desired batch. Please inform us at least one week in advance. There's no additional charge for batch transfers.

Q: What if I miss a class?

We provide class recordings for online batches. For offline classes, you can attend a make-up session in another batch or receive notes from your instructor. We encourage regular attendance for best results.

Q: Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer early bird discounts (register 2 weeks before batch starts), sibling discounts (10% for family members), and group discounts (5% for groups of 3+). Full payment upfront receives 5% discount.

Q: Will I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, all students who complete the course with at least 80% attendance receive a course completion certificate from Wave Language Club. This certificate can be useful for academic and professional purposes.

Q: What is the refund policy?

Full refund if you cancel before the course starts. After the course begins, refunds are calculated on a pro-rata basis minus a 10% administrative fee. Refunds processed within 14 business days.
